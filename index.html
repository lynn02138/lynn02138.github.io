I am a researcher specializing in the application of computational methods to East Asian history. My work focuses on the intersection of large-scale text analysis and traditional archival research, aiming to uncover hidden patterns in historical data.

目前，我是哈佛大學的博士候選人。我的論文探討了清代知識交流的數字圖譜，我熱衷於開放科學、數字教學以及歷史研究中的數據倫理。

Currently, I am a PhD Candidate at Harvard University. My dissertation explores the digital mapping of knowledge exchange during the Qing Dynasty. I am passionate about open science, digital pedagogy, and the ethics of data representation in historical research.
